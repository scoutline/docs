For teams building on or with Scoutline, the focus is on **Developer Velocity**. We provide the tools and environments needed to build robust legal solutions without touching live case data.

## Strictly Typed Ecosystem

The Scoutline codebase is strictly typed using **TypeScript** across the entire stack. This prevents runtime errors in critical legal functions and ensures a predictable development experience.

- **SDKs**: We provide SDKs for popular languages to simplify integration with Cognito, S3, and our core API.
- **Shared Schemas**: Use our published type definitions to ensure data compatibility.

## The Sandbox Environment

We provide a dedicated "Sandbox" environment where developers can test Agentic Lawyer workflows and API integrations.
- **Simulated Case Data**: Test your logic against high-fidelity, anonymized legal scenarios.
- **Risk-Free Testing**: Trigger document drafting and vault movements without affecting live production resources.
- **Audit Logging**: Debug your integrations using the same granular audit trails available in production.

## Integrated Workflow

We optimize for high-stakes environments:
- **Linear Integration**: Track feature requests and bug reports through our integrated project management board.
- **Slack Community**: Get real-time support and feedback from the Scoutline core team.
- **Fast Deployments**: Our AWS-centric CI/CD pipeline ensures that your custom integrations are deployed with minimal latency.
