The Scoutline Canvas maintains a complete, point-in-time record of your case strategy evolution.

## Strategy Snapshots

Scoutline automatically takes snapshots of the Canvas after major modifications or before sharing.
- **Compare Mode**: Overlay two strategy snapshots to see how your case theory has evolved over time.
- **Attribution**: Hover over any node change to see which team member performed the modification and at what timestamp.

## Reversion Logic

Firms can revert the entire canvas or individual node branches to previous states, ensuring that no discarded theory is ever truly lost. Even reverted nodes remain searchable in the historical audit trail.
