Every legal matter is unique. The Scoutline Canvas provides modular workspace layouts designed to reduce cognitive load and maximize drafting speed.

## Layout Presets

| Layout | Focus | Ideal For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strategy First** | Timeline & Graph | Early-stage case mapping. |
| **Evidence Dense** | Document Preview & Vault | Discovery review and fact-checking. |
| **Drafting Focus** | Split Editor | High-speed filing generation. |

## Customizing Your View

- **Resizable Panes**: Drag borders to prioritize specific tools.
- **Node Hiding**: Collapse irrelevant entity branches to stay focused.
- **Pinned Views**: Lock specific evidence nodes to the corner of your screen.

## Full-Screen Mode

Enter a distraction-free environment for deep strategy sessions by pressing `Cmd + .`
