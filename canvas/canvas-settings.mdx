Customize your workspace to match your firm's "Interface Craft" preferences.

## UI Customization

- **Theme**: Toggle between Pure Black and High-Density Gray modes.
- **Node Density**: Adjust the default spacing and text size of entity nodes.
- **Grid Settings**: Enable/disable snapping for precise node alignment.

## Performance Profiles

- **Standard**: Balanced animations and rendering.
- **High Performance**: Disables micro-animations for extremely large node graphs (1000+ entities).
