---
title: "Canvas Security"
description: "Role-based access for visual intelligence data"
---

Visual intelligence is highly sensitive. Scoutline provides granular access controls for the Intelligence Canvas.

## Permission Tiers

- **Viewer**: Can see the nodes and links but cannot modify the graph.
- **Contributor**: Can add nodes and propose links (subject to review).
- **Editor**: Full control over clusters, layers, and exports.
- **Admin**: Can manage sharing permissions and delete version snapshots.

## Perimeter Isolation

Canvas data resides within your firm's private AWS or Self-Hosted perimeter, protected by the same KMS encryption as your Vault assets.
