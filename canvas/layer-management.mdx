The Scoutline Canvas utilizes a sophisticated layering system to ensure that your legal strategy remains clean while staying deeply connected to the underlying evidence.

## The Three-Layer System

1. **Evidence Layer (Base)**: The ground truth. Hardened documents, transcripts, and metadata.
2. **Strategy Layer**: Your legal theories, nodes of interest, and causal links.
3. **Execution Layer**: The drafting progress, citations, and court-ready output.

## Toggling Visibility

Use the **Layer Palette** to hide or reveal specific case dimensions. This is critical for:
- Redacting sensitive layers before internal sharing.
- Switching between "Plaintiff Vision" and "Defense Rebuttal" views.

## Cross-Layer Referencing

Nodes on the Strategy Layer can be "anchored" to specific coordinates or text snippets in the Evidence Layer, creating a permanent, verifiable link.
