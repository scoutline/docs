Temporal intelligence is critical in litigation. The Canvas features an integrated Dynamic Timeline that syncs with your Case Nodes and the Vault.

## Automated Chronology

Our Agentic Parser scans your Vault documents and automatically populates the timeline with:
- **Key Events**: Identified from deposition transcripts and email headers.
- **Filing Deadlines**: Derived from court rules and case schedules.

## Navigating Time

- **Zoom**: Scale the timeline from years down to individual hours.
- **Filter**: Isolate events related to a specific Entity Node.
- **Branching**: Create alternative "Theory Timelines" to test different case strategies.

## Verification

Every point on the timeline is a link. Clicking an event takes you directly to the source document and the exact snippet where the date/time was verified.
