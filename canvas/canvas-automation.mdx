The Scoutline Canvas is an active participant in your legal drafting. You can trigger complex agentic workflows directly from the visual workspace.

## Context-Aware Drafting

By selecting a cluster of nodes (e.g., a Defendant node and several Fact/Evidence nodes) and choosing **"Draft Rebuttal,"** our agents will:
1. Extract the relationship metadata from the canvas.
2. Fetch the linked documents from the Vault.
3. Generate a strictly-typed legal draft that reflects the visual strategy.

## Autonomous Research

Right-click any Entity Node and select **"Discover Connections."** Scoutline will search the Vault and jurisdictional databases for unmapped relationships, automatically proposing new nodes and links for your review.
