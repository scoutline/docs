The Scoutline Intelligence Canvas is not just a document editor; it is a spatial workspace where legal strategy, evidence, and relational data converge into a single "Interface Craft" experience.

## The Vision: Spatial Intelligence

Traditional legal tools are linear. The Canvas is multi-dimensional, allowing you to:
- **Visualize Connections**: Map the relationship between entities, evidence, and statutes.
- **Agentic Interaction**: Interact with your case data using natural language directly on the workspace.
- **Verifiable Strategy**: Every node on the canvas is pinned to a hardened source in the Vault.

## Core Pillars

1. **High-Density Workspace**: Every pixel is optimized for professional utility.
2. **Real-Time Collaboration**: Peer-to-peer strategy with zero latency.
3. **Drafting Integration**: One-click generation of court-ready filings from canvas nodes.
