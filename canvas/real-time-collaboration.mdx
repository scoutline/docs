description: "Peer-to-peer strategy sessions with zero latency"
---

Scoutline enables senior partners and associates to collaborate on the Intelligence Canvas in real-time, utilizing a secure peer-to-peer synchronization engine.

## Collaborative Features

- **Live Presence**: See where your colleagues are focused on the canvas.
- **Shared Nodes**: Modifications to the entity graph are visible across all participating firm members instantly.
- **Voice Overlays**: Start a temporary audio channel for rapid strategy alignment during discovery review.

## Version Control

Despite being real-time, every change is tracked. You can revert to previous canvas states or see which associate added a specific Link Node.

## Conflict Resolution

In the rare event of simultaneous modifications to a single node, Scoutline utilizes a legal-centric priority logic (e.g., senior partner overrides) or prompts for a manual merger of the theories.
