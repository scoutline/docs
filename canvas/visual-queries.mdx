Query your case strategy using natural language via the Canvas Intelligence panel.

## Sample Queries

- *"Show me all nodes linked to the Plaintiff that contradict Witness A."*
- *"Highlight the most cited evidence nodes in the current strategy."*
- *"Are there any fact nodes on the timeline missing verifiable sources?"*

## Intelligent Highlighting

The agent doesn't just answer; it **highlights** the relevant nodes and links on the canvas, allowing you to visually grasp the answer's context within the broader case structure.
