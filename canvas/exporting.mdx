When your strategy is finalized, the Intelligence Canvas can be exported into various formats required by standard legal procedures.

## Export Formats

- **PDF Evidence Map**: A high-resolution visualization of your case theory, including deep links to cited documents.
- **Structured Draft (MDX/DOCX)**: A comprehensive text representation of the nodes and links, pre-formatted for court submission.
- **Audit Reports**: A CSV/JSON breakdown of every assertion on the canvas and its verifiable source.

## Metadata Integrity

All exports include hidden cryptographic signatures that verify the data originated from your hardened Scoutline perimeter, ensuring the "Interface Craft" quality is maintained in the final output.
