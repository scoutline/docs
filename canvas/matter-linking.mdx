---
title: "Matter Linking"
description: "Connecting canvas state to specific legal matters"
---

To maintain professional organization, every Intelligence Canvas is linked to a specific Matter ID within your firm's Scoutline environment.

## Contextual Isolation

By linking a canvas to a matter, the agentic models gain focused access to:
- **Matter-Specific Vaults**: Only documents relevant to the case are suggested for node creation.
- **Strictly-Typed Templates**: Drafting tools prioritize the jurisdictional rules defined in the matter metadata.

## Switching Matters

You can "Clone" a canvas strategy to a new matter, allowing you to reuse successful strategy graphs across similar cases while maintaining data isolation between different clients.
