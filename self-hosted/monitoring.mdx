Maintaining the health of your self-hosted perimeter is critical for ensuring consistent legal outcome delivery.

## Health Check Endpoint

Monitor the `sc-core` health check endpoint for real-time status:

```bash
curl http://localhost:8080/health
```

## Prometheus Integration

Scoutline exports performance metrics in a Prometheus-compatible format. You can visualize:
- **Drafting Latency**: Time taken per agentic job.
- **Memory Pressure**: Utilization of the Vector DB and core containers.
- **Error Rates**: Tracking "Audit-Ready" workflow success.

## Error-Proof Tracing

Integrated tracing allows your infrastructure team to identify bottlenecking services before they affect the lawyers' "Interface Craft" experience.
