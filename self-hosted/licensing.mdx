Self-hosted deployments require a valid Scoutline Enterprise License Key to unlock the full potential of the Agentic Lawyer and jurisdictional search.

## Activation Flow

1. **Obtain Key**: Retrieve your license key from the Scoutline Enterprise Portal.
2. **Inject Key**: Add the key to your `.env` file as `SCOUTLINE_LICENSE_KEY`.
3. **Verification**: During startup, the Bootstrap service validates the key against our Global API.

## Seat Management

Self-hosted instances allow for flexible seat management within the firm's private perimeter. The internal admin dashboard provides granular controls for:
- **User Provisioning**: Manually adding or syncing legal team members via LDAP/AD.
- **Role Assignment**: Defining access levels for the Vault and Intelligence Canvas.

## Success-Case Fees

Even in self-hosted environments, automated filings and major drafting jobs are subject to successful outcome-based fees, calculated and reported via the encrypted telemetry link.
