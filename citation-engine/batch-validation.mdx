The Scoutline Citation Engine can perform high-density batch validation across a firm's entire document repository.

## Automated Auditing

Run a batch validation job to:
1. **Identify Unverified Claims**: Scan thousands of discovery documents for assertions missing verifiable sources.
2. **Auto-Map Entities**: Connect disparate documents mentioning the same parties or facts.
3. **Draft Readiness**: Mark documents as "Verified" once all internal claims have passed the engine's integrity checks.

## Performance Profile

Batch validation utilizes the Scoutline Agentic Cluster, allowing for the processing of up to 10,000 pages per hour while maintaining the "Interface Craft" precision required for legal audit trails.
