The Scoutline Citation Engine is a "strictly-typed" system that adheres to the most rigorous legal formatting standards.

## The Rule Engine

Our engine incorporates thousands of individual Bluebook rules, including:
- **Case Names**: Automatic abbreviation and italicization.
- **Pinpoint Citations**: Handling diverse page and paragraph numbering schemes from different court reporters.
- **Signals & Parentheticals**: Ensuring that complex explanatory parentheticals match the semantic context of the draft.

## ALWD Support

For academic or specific jurisdictional requirements, the engine can be toggled to ALWD mode, ensuring that every period and comma is placed with precision.
