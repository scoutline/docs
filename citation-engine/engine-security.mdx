The Citation Engine and its underlying data graph are protected by the same "Zero-Trust" architecture as the rest of the Scoutline Legal OS.

## Integrity Hardening

- **Source Integrity**: Every document in the Vault is SHA-256 hashed to prevent source-tampering before verification.
- **Isolation**: The citation logic runs within a strictly-managed containerized environment, ensuring that firm strategy never leaks between matters.
- **Encrypted Trails**: Audit logs are KMS-encrypted at rest and only accessible via authenticated Cognito JWT holders.
