Every verified claim in Scoutline is mapped directly to a source in the **Hardened S3 Vault**.

## Direct Mapping Logic

When the Citation Engine identifies a supporting document, it creates a Cryptographic Link between:
- **The Assertion**: The specific text in your motion or brief.
- **The Source**: The exact page, paragraph, and line number in the discovery document or legal statute.

## Visual Verification

In the Scoutline UI, hovering over a citation brings up the **Source Map Pane**, which displays the original document snippet in real-time, side-by-side with your writing.

## Immutable References

Because your Vault is hardened with WORM (Write Once, Read Many) policies, the Source Map is immutable. Your citations will never "break" or point to modified data.
