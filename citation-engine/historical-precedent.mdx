The Citation Engine understands that the law is not static. It provides deep-temporal tracing for every cited statute and case.

## Temporal Mapping

- **Law at the Time of Event**: Verifies if a citation was valid during the specific period relevant to the matter.
- **Subsequent History**: Automatically flags if a cited case has been overruled, distinguished, or called into question since your last draft was generated.

## Precedent Alerts

If a binding precedent changes during your litigation cycle, the engine will send an immediate **Perimeter Alert** on the Intelligence Canvas, allowing your strategy to pivot before the next filing.
