---
title: "Verifiability Checks"
description: "Integrity verification for legal and factual sources"
---

Before the Citation Engine allows a source to be used, it performs a series of high-density verifiability checks.

## Checks Performed

1. **Source Authenticity**: Verifies the document origin and cryptographic signature.
2. **Contextual Integrity**: Ensures the cited snippet hasn't been taken out of context by analyzing the surrounding 500 words.
3. **Temporal Validity**: Checks if the cited law was in effect at the time of the event and remains valid today.

## Verification Tiers

| Tier | Status | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gold** | Fully Verified | Direct semantic match with signed source. |
| **Silver** | Inferred | High-probability match requires partner review. |
| **Red** | Unverified | Hallucination detected or missing source. |
