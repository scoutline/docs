description: "Mapping case law and court rules across jurisdictions"

The Scoutline Citation Engine understands the complex hierarchy of legal authority across different jurisdictions.

## Jurisdictional Awareness

The engine automatically adapts its verification logic based on the matter's jurisdiction:
- **Federal vs. State**: Prioritizes the correct hierarchy of precedent.
- **Circuit-Specific Rules**: Adjusts citation formatting and local rule compliance.

## Precedent Mapping

When citations are pulled from different jurisdictions, the engine clarifies the "Weight of Authority" (e.g., Binding vs. Persuasive), allowing the lawyer to make informed strategy decisions on the Intelligence Canvas.
