Transformation from drafting to court-ready filings includes the generation of high-density citation metadata.

## Citation Embedding

When you export a document from Scoutline, the Citation Engine embeds:
- **Interactive Links**: Allowing court clerks or opposing counsel to verify the sources (if permissions are granted).
- **Verification Signatures**: Cryptographic proof that the citations were verified against the firm's private Vault.

## Tables of Authorities (TOA)

The engine automatically generates a comprehensive Table of Authorities, correctly grouped by:
- Statutes
- Cases
- Other Authorities

This ensures that the final filing meets the high-fidelity "Interface Craft" standards required for professional legal output.
