Stop manual formatting. The Scoutline Citation Engine handles the heavy lifting of legal citation styling.

## Supported Styles

- **Bluebook**: Standard for most US legal proceedings.
- **ALWD**: Advanced legal writing directory standards.
- **Local Court Rules**: Specific formatting required by individual judges or circuits.

## Intelligent Styling

The engine doesn't just format text; it understands the citation's role:
- **Short Form**: Automatically detects if a full citation has already been used and switches to *id.* or short-form citations.
- **Signal Logic**: Proposes signals like *see also*, *cf.*, or *accord* based on the semantic relationship identified by the agent.
