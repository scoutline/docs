title: "Citation Graph Exports"
description: "JSON/XML export of verifiability data"
---

Export the complete citation and verifiability graph of your matter for use in external analytics or disclosure procedures.

## Export Schemas

- **JSON Graph**: A machine-readable representation of all nodes (assertions) and links (citations).
- **Compliance XML**: Standardized format for court-ready metadata submission.

## Use Cases

- **Litigation Analytics**: Importing your citation graph into specialized statistics tools to identify strategy patterns.
- **Disclosure Packages**: Providing opposing counsel or the court with a verifiable map of your evidence-assertion links.
