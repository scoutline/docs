title: "Audit Trail Sync"
description: "Syncing assertions with firm compliance logs"
---

Every verified citation is recorded in the Scoutline Audit Trail, providing a permanent record of the "Verifiable Intelligence" used in your filings.

## Real-Time Logging

When a citation is validated, the engine logs:
- **Timestamp**: Exact time of verification.
- **User ID**: The lawyer or associate who proposed the assertion.
- **Source Hash**: A cryptographic signature of the source document at the time of verification.

## Compliance Exports

Firms can export these logs for internal quality control or to satisfy regulatory requirements concerning the use of AI in legal discovery and drafting.

## Perimeter Integration

Audit logs are stored within your private perimeter, ensuring that your firm's strategy evolution remains confidential and audit-ready.
