---
title: "Integrity Reports"
description: "Generating verification summaries for senior partners"
---

Before a final filing is submitted, the Citation Engine can generate an **Integrity Report** that summarizes the "Verifiable Intelligence" within the document.

## Report Components

- **Verification Density**: Percentage of assertions successfully mapped to Vault sources.
- **Signal Summary**: A breakdown of the legal signals used (e.g., *Accord*, *See Also*).
- **Red Flags**: A list of any Silver or Red tier citations requiring senior partner review.

## Partner Sign-off

Integrity Reports provide senior partners with the confidence that the work prepared by associates and agentic models is factually and legally sound, significantly reducing the "Review Overhead" in busy firms.
