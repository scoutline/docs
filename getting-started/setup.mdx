Setting up a firm on Scoutline is designed to be frictionless. We prioritize a "zero-touch" implementation philosophy that isolates your data while providing a customized environment.

## Firm Subdomain Strategy

Every firm on Scoutline is isolated via a dedicated subdomain structure. This ensures:
- **Data Isolation**: Your firm's environment is physically and logically separated from others.
- **Customized Branding**: Tailor the interface to match your firm's identity.
- **Dedicated Perimeter**: Independent AWS security groups and IAM policies per firm.

## Implementation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initialize Foundation">
    Run the Scoutline activation service to provision your dedicated AWS resources (Cognito, S3 Vault, RDS).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Repositories">
    Integrate your existing document repositories (OneDrive, Google Workspace, or legacy databases) with the Scoutline Vault.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agentic Indexing">
    Our Agentic Lawyer begins indexing your firm's specific legal context, mapping precedents and case structures.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Canvas">
    Invite your team to the dedicated firm dashboard and begin collaborating on the Intelligence Canvas.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Audit-Ready from Day One

The system is built to be "audit-ready" immediately upon deployment. Pre-configured templates for common legal workflows ensure your firm meets compliance standards without manual configuration.
