Security is the foundation of Scoutline, not an afterthought. The **Vault** is a dedicated, high-security storage environment for sensitive discovery documents and client communications.

## Hardened S3 Architecture

Our storage layer is engineered exclusively on **AWS S3**, utilizing multiple layers of protection:
- **KMS Encryption**: All data is encrypted at rest using AWS Key Management Service with customer-managed keys.
- **Strict IAM Policies**: Granular Identity and Access Management (IAM) roles ensure that only authorized services and users can access specific objects.
- **Data Sovereignty**: Choose the AWS region that meets your firm's specific geographical and regulatory requirements.

## Document Indexing

Once documents are moved into the Vault, the Agentic Lawyer begins indexing and mapping the content. This process provides:
- **Instant Search**: Find any keyword or concept across millions of pages in seconds.
- **Semantic Mapping**: Understand the relationships between different case files.
- **Integrity Checks**: Automated verification of document metadata and audit logs.
