Scoutline operates on a high-margin automation model designed to align our incentives with the success of the law firms we serve.

## Automation-Based Pricing

Unlike traditional software that charges per seat, Scoutline's model is built around value and outcomes:
- **Lifetime Activation Fee**: A one-time fee to initialize your firm's dedicated AWS perimeter.
- **Success Case Fees**: Flexible pricing based on successful filings and automated tasks.

## The Error-Proof Guarantee

We are so confident in our **Verifiable Intelligence** that we offer a unique "Skin in the Game" guarantee:
- **Financial Compensation**: Scoutline provides financial compensation for system-generated inaccuracies.
- **Accuracy Alignment**: Our incentives are directly tied to the precision and success of your legal filings.

## Firm Growth

By reducing manual overhead and fragmented tooling, Scoutline enables firms to scale their output without linear increases in headcount, turning the platform into a revenue-generating asset rather than a cost center.
