Scoutline’s feature set is built around the concept of **Agentic Lawyering**, turning the platform into a multi-dimensional workspace for the modern lawyer.

## Intelligence Canvas

Unlike traditional text editors, the Intelligence Canvas is a dynamic workspace where lawyers can:
- **Visualize Case Structures**: Map out complex legal relationships and timelines.
- **Cross-Reference Evidence**: Link every claim to its source in real-time.
- **Traceable Intelligence**: Every piece of information is verifiable, ensuring that legal arguments are built on facts.

## Agentic Drafting

The system can autonomously generate complex legal filings, briefs, and contracts based on user-provided case data. It doesn't just "fill templates"—it understands the context of the case to produce high-quality drafts.

## Interactive Chat

A specialized interface for querying case files, statutes, and internal precedents using natural language. 
- Ask questions like *"What are the precedents for liability in this jurisdiction?"*
- Retrieve specific document citations instantly.

## Audit-Ready Workflows

Every action taken within the platform—from a document edit to an AI-generated draft—is logged with a granular audit trail. This ensures that your firm meets all regulatory and compliance requirements with zero extra effort.
